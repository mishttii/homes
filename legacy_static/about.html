About Pluto Home

Our Story

Pluto Home was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform everyday living spaces into havens of comfort and style. What started as a small venture has grown into a trusted brand, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

We believe that your home is more than just a place to live—it's a reflection of your personality and a sanctuary from the outside world. That's why we're committed to providing the highest quality home furnishing products at affordable prices.
